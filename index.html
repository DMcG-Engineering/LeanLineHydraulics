Hydraulic Hoses

Made-to-Order Hydraulic Hose Assemblies

Bring your old hose, a drawing or your thread and length details. We build hose assemblies to your specifications and also supply hose and fittings individually.

Custom Hose Assemblies

We help you select the correct hose type, size and pressure rating for your application, then crimp the matching ends ready for installation.

  • High-pressure braided and spiral hoses
  • Suction and return hose options
  • Assemblies built to exact length
  • Ideal for mobile and industrial hydraulics

Hose & Fittings Sold Individually

Prefer to make your own hoses? We supply loose hose, ferrules and hose ends so you can assemble on your own equipment.

  • Loose hose by the metre
  • Ferrules for 1-wire, 2-wire and multi-spiral
  • BSP, JIC, ORFS, NPT, Metric hose ends
  • Straight, 45°, 90° and tee configurations
Hydraulic Components

Hydraulic Fittings, Valves & Motors

Everything from simple adapters through to control valves and hydraulic motors to keep your machines moving.

Hose Fittings & Adapters

Carbon-steel fittings and adapters to suit common hydraulic thread standards used in Australian industry.

  • BSP, JIC, ORFS, NPT and Metric threads
  • Straight, elbow and tee fittings
  • Bulkhead and reducer fittings
  • Caps and plugs for service and transport

Hydraulic Valves

Control flow and pressure in your circuit with a range of directional and line-mounted valves.

  • Directional control valves
  • Check and pilot-operated check valves
  • Flow control and needle valves
  • Relief and pressure control valves

Hydraulic Motors

Low-speed, high-torque motors for driven equipment in agriculture, construction and industrial applications.

  • Various displacements and speeds
  • Multiple mounting and shaft options
  • Suited to augers, conveyors, brushes and more
  • Matched to your flow and pressure
Pneumatic Components

Pneumatic Fittings, Cylinders & Air Preparation

A complete pneumatics offering for factory automation, general machinery and industrial air systems.

Air Preparation (FRLs)

Protect downstream equipment and stabilise air pressure with filter, regulator and lubricator units.

  • Filter–regulator combinations
  • Stand-alone filters and regulators
  • Lubricators for extended valve and tool life
  • Modular designs for flexible layouts

Pneumatic Cylinders

Cylinders for automation and general industrial use, in a range of bore sizes and strokes.

  • Standard ISO/profile cylinders
  • Compact and short-stroke options
  • Mounting hardware and rod accessories
  • Suitable for new builds and replacements

Fittings, Tubing & Valves

Build and control your air circuits with fittings, tube and directional control valves.

  • Push-in and threaded fittings
  • Polyurethane and nylon tubing
  • Solenoid and manual directional valves
  • Manifolds for compact valve banks
