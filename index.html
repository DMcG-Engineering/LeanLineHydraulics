Product Range

Core Hydraulic & Pneumatic Categories

These are the main groups of components LeanLine focuses on. Your quote can cover single parts, hose assemblies or complete kits.

Hydraulic Hoses & Fittings

High-pressure hose, adaptors and hose ends to suit common thread standards used in Australian industry.

  • Wire-braided and spiral high-pressure hoses
  • BSP, JIC, ORFS, NPT and metric ends
  • Loose hose & fittings or made-to-length assemblies

Valves, Controls & Flow Devices

Directional, over-centre and check valves plus flow dividers and relief valves to control your circuits.

  • Double over-centre & counterbalance valves
  • Pilot-operated check valves & cross-relief valves
  • Flow dividers for multi-cylinder or multi-motor systems

Motors & Pneumatics

Low-speed high-torque hydraulic motors plus pneumatic cylinders, valves and air preparation units.

  • Orbital motors for drives and augers
  • Pneumatic ISO cylinders & valve manifolds
  • Filters, regulators and lubricators (FRLs)
Sample Quote Items

Typical Hydraulic Components We Quote

These examples show the sort of hydraulic parts LeanLine can supply. You can reference the codes below when you send through your enquiry.

LL-HOSE-2SN-01
High-Pressure Steel Wire Braided Hose

High-pressure hydraulic hose suitable for mobile and industrial applications. Available in multiple sizes and pressure ratings.

LL-VALVE-CBV-06
Double Counterbalance / Over-Centre Valve

Counterbalance valve for load holding on cylinders and motors, with adjustable relief settings for smooth, safe motion.

LL-MOTOR-LSHT-315
Low-Speed High-Torque Orbital Motor

LSHT motor for augers, conveyors and rotating equipment. Multiple displacements and mount options available.

LL-FITTING-BSP-60C
BSP Female 60° Cone Seat Fitting

BSP female 60° cone seat fitting for hose assemblies into BSP ports. Commonly used on mobile and industrial hydraulic systems.

Pneumatic Components

Pneumatic Air Preparation & Actuators

LeanLine can also supply a range of pneumatic components for factory automation and general industrial use – including FRL units and ISO-profile cylinders.

Air Preparation (FRLs)

Filter–regulator–lubricator units to protect downstream equipment and stabilise air pressure.

  • Filter–regulator combinations and stand-alone units
  • Gauge options and auto drain variants
  • Modular designs for flexible layouts

DSBC-Style Pneumatic Cylinders

ISO-profile pneumatic cylinders suitable for automation and general machinery.

  • Range of bore sizes and strokes
  • Adjustable cushioning and sensor options
  • Mounting brackets and rod accessories available

Fittings, Tubing & Valves

Push-in and threaded fittings, polyurethane and nylon tubing plus solenoid and manual valves for building your air circuits.

  • Manifolds for compact valve banks
  • Quick-connect fittings for easy assembly
  • Options suited to factory and onsite use
